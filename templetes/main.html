What's Geomagnatic Storm

A geomagnetic storm is a major disturbance of Earth's magnetosphere that occurs when there is a very efficient exchange of energy from the solar wind into the space environment surrounding Earth.

Why it's Danger

Geomagnatic storms make it diffcult or impossible and global-positioning system (GPS) communications can be degraded.

satellite electronics can be damaged through the build up and discharge of static-electric charges. Astronauts and high altitude pilots can be subjected to increased levels of radiation.

they make high voltage surges in power grids that can make breakouts.

What indicates

Geomagnatic storms can be measuremed / detected through Kp index of earth. Kp index can be predicted from DSCOVR data.

The Challenge

The challange is to develop a machine learning pipeline to predict the probability of the solar storm event.

Our Approach

Our approach involves transforming DSCOVR raw data into more relevant information for kp prediction, with a focus on employing neural networks. Our proposed steps to implement this solution are as follows: (1)Preprocess DSCOVR raw data and convert it into more structured formats. (2)Train a model connecting DSCOVR data with WIND data. (3)Develop a forecasting model. (4)Train the main model using data from the two models to predict the kp index. (5)Create a user-friendly website for easy access to predicted kp index information for upcoming days.
